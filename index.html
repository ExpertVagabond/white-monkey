FAQ

Common questions.

What types of projects do you work with?
We work with Layer-1/2 protocols, DeFi platforms, NFT projects, infrastructure providers, and any Web3 venture looking to enter or expand in Asian markets. Whether you're pre-launch or already live, we tailor our approach to your stage.
How long does a typical engagement last?
Most engagements start with a 3-month sprint covering strategy, initial market entry, and first exchange listings. Many clients continue on retainer for ongoing community management, KOL activations, and market expansion.
Do you help with Chinese regulatory compliance?
Yes. We structure market entry to comply with local regulations across all our covered jurisdictions — including mainland China's unique landscape, Hong Kong's SFC framework, Singapore's MAS guidelines, and more. We work with local legal counsel in each market.
What makes White Monkey different from other consultancies?
We're operators, not slide deck factories. Every team member has hands-on experience in Asian crypto markets. We have direct relationships — not introductions to introductions. When we say we'll get you listed, we have the BD contacts to make it happen.
What are your fees?
We offer flexible engagement models: project-based fees for specific deliverables (exchange listing, token launch), monthly retainers for ongoing market development, and success-based structures for select partnerships. Book a call to discuss what fits your needs.
